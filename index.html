Lukas Huber

Lukas combines 15 years practical investment experience with profound knowledge of financial markets, asset classes, and instruments. His focus is on advising wealthy entrepreneurs and family offices on institutional asset management solutions and professional banking infrastructure. In this role he held positions at Credit Suisse UHNW, UBS UHNW, and LGT. Lukas graduated from the Mathematic - Scientific Gymnasium in Zurich and holds a master’s degree in law from the University of Zurich.

Dr. Alexandre Ziegler

Alex combines 20 years of academic research in finance with practical portfolio management experience. He has been the director of the Center for Portfolio Management at the University of Zurich since 2015. His courses at the University of Zurich include asset pricing, portfolio management, and risk management. In addition, he has 10 years practical experience in the implementation of systematic investment strategies. Alex graduated from Collège Calvin (Latin, Greek, Mathematic-Scientific) in Geneva and holds an MBA from Stanford University and a Ph.D. in Finance from the University of St. Gallen.
